How it works
  1. Captures 8 seconds of frontal video from your webcam.
  2. Uses MediaPipe FaceMesh to extract facial landmarks in real time.
  3. Computes a simple mouth-asymmetry score and eye/gaze asymmetry as screening signals.
Important: This is an experimental screening tool for development/demo only. If you suspect stroke, call emergency services immediately.
Built with MediaPipe FaceMesh. Source available on GitHub.
